Room for adjustments

The S 1000 R comes with two standard riding modes” “Rain” and “Road.” 
The ABS and ASC in “Rain mode” provides a more gentle throttle, best suited for wet road surfaces; while the “Road” mode optimizes the throttle response of the ABS and ASC when riding on dry asphalt.
